Welcome to the Marvel Arena!

Choose your hero!

Iron Man

Iron Man possesses powered armor that gives him superhuman strength and durability, flight, and an array of weapons.

Spider-Man

Spider-Man's powers include superhuman strength, a "spidey-sense" that alerts him to danger, perfect balance and equilibrium, as well as superhuman speed and agility.

Storm

Storm is one of the most powerful mutants on Earth and has demonstrated a plethora of abilities. Storm possesses the psionic ability to control all forms of weather over vast areas.

Thor

Thor has superhuman speed, agility, and reflexes, enabling him to deflect bullets with his hammer, and to swing or throw it at many times the speed of light.

Hulk

Hulk possesses incredible superhuman strength, durability, and healing factor. He becomes more powerful as anger increases

Cyclops

Cyclops can emit powerful beams of energy from his eyes. He cannot control the beams without the aid of special eyewear which he must wear at all times.

Phoenix (Jean Grey)

While empowered by the Phoenix Force, Jean can create and control flames at will, control and manipulate pure matter, energy, and thoughts completely.

Wolverine

Wolverine possesses animal-keen senses, enhanced physical capabilities, a powerful regenerative ability known as a healing factor, and three retractable claws in each hand.
